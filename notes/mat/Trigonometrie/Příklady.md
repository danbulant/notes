a)
$a=10cm$
$\alpha=62\degree$
$\beta=34\degree$
$\gamma=180-\alpha-\beta=84\degree$

$\frac{a}{\sin\alpha}=\frac{b}{\sin\beta}$
$b=\frac{a}{\sin\alpha}\sin\beta$
$b=\frac{10}{\sin62}\sin34$
$b\dot=6.33$

$\frac{a}{\sin\alpha}=\frac{c}{\sin\gamma}$
$c=\frac{a}{\sin\alpha}\sin\gamma$
$c=\frac{10}{\sin62}\sin84$
$c\dot=11.2$
